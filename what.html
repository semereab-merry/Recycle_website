What Is Recycling?

How often do we hear the word recycling? Probably a lot. However, we just know that we should recycle and sometimes we may not know what you can contribute so that the green environment will be achieved

Let's start with the common question. What is recycling? The video below explains briefly about recycling.



Recycling is the process of converting waste materials into new materials and objects. The recyclability of a material depends on its ability to reacquire the properties it had in its virgin or original state.


There are several types of recycling:

  • plastic recycling
  • glass recycling
  • paper recycling
  • domestic waste recycling
  • metal recycling
  • WEEE resycling
  • and textile resycling
